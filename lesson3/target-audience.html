Target Audience and Scenarios

Personas


Creator and Manager of MariaCare

Fictional name: Maria Rossi

Demographics: 50 years old, married with 2 children

Goals and tasks: She would like to meet other entrepreneurs who work like her a lot with the internet and have information to grow her business.

Environment: She has a store in town and online. She works with her daughter. Most of its customers are online. She uses the internet on a daily basis and answers the calls of her clients and future clients.


Future Entrepeneur

Fictional name: Luis Sanchez

Demographics: 30 years old, single

Goals and tasks: He arrived from Peru in Italy 8 years ago. He has done a lot of work and now wants to start his own business thanks to the savings he has made.He wants to get information, help, and meet people who have gone through or are going through the same journey as him.

Environment: He has a mobile phone and a good quality internet subscription. He surfs the internet very well.
