Historical Context

Quote

"The power of the Web is in its universality. Access by everyone regardless of disability is an essential aspect." by W3C Director and inventor of the World Wide Web, Tim Berners-Lee

Origins

"The Web is fundamentally designed to work for all people, whatever their hardware, software, language, location, or ability. When the Web meets this goal, it is accessible to people with a diverse range of hearing, movement, sight, and cognitive ability."
Extract from w3.org

Chronology


  • 1990 - Creation of the world Wilde Web (www)
  • 1994 - Foundation of The World Wide Web Consortium (W3C)
  • 1997 - Official Launch of the Web Accessibility Initiative (WAI)
